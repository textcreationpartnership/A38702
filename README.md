DEFENCE OF
IOHN ETHERINGTON
Against STEPHEN DENISON,
and his witnesses, their accusations,
and depositions: upon which he was
censured by the high Commission
Court.

      * The deposition of Thomson.

      * The deposition of Rogers.

      * The deposition of Nicholson.
